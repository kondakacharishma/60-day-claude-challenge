# Day 22 – Startup Validation Report using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use Claude AI as a Startup Advisor, VC Analyst, and Market Research Expert to validate a startup idea and generate a professional Startup Validation Report.

---

## 💡 Startup Idea

An AI-powered platform that helps students and fresh graduates with personalized career guidance, resume optimization, interview preparation, and skill development.
