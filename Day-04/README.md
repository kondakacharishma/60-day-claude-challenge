# Day 4 – Chain of Thought Prompting

## Objective
Learn how Chain of Thought prompting improves AI reasoning and generate a personalized AI learning roadmap.

## Activity 1
Used Chain of Thought prompting to solve a problem step by step.

## Activity 2
Generated a personalized AI learning roadmap by answering four questions:
- Education: B.Tech Student
- Skills: Python, Java, Cybersecurity Basics, HTML, CSS, GitHub
- Career Goal: AI Engineer and Cybersecurity Professional
- Learning Duration: 1 year

## What I Learned
- Chain of Thought prompting improves reasoning quality.
- Breaking problems into smaller steps leads to better AI responses.
- A personalized roadmap helps structure long-term AI learning.
