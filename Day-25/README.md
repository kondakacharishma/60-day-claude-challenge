# Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use Claude AI to generate a complete AI Shark Tank Simulator as a single self-contained HTML application. The simulator evaluates startup ideas by simulating investor interactions, asking business-related questions, and providing an investment decision based on multiple evaluation criteria.

---

## 💡 Startup Idea

**AI Career Mentor**

An AI-powered platform that provides personalized career guidance, resume optimization, interview preparation, and skill development recommendations for students and fresh graduates.

---

## 🚀 Features Implemented

### 📝 Startup Input

- Startup Name
- Problem Statement
- Solution
- Revenue Model
- Target Audience
- Funding Ask

---

### 🦈 AI Shark Tank Panel

Four AI investor personas evaluated the startup:

- Venture Capitalist
- Founder
- Customer
- Angel Investor

Each judge asked business-focused questions related to market potential, execution, customer value, and profitability.

---

### 📊 Startup Evaluation

The simulator generated scores for:

- Market Potential
- Innovation
- Business Model
- Execution
- Investment Worthiness

---

### 💰 Investment Decision

The application provided:

- Investment Recommendation
- Suggested Company Valuation
- Funding Recommendation
- Detailed Business Reasoning

---

### 🏆 Additional Features
