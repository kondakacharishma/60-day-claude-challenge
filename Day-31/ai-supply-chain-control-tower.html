How to Play

You are the Head of Operations for a global supply chain. Disruption alerts will appear on your dashboard — ports, suppliers, trucks, warehouses, customs, demand spikes and more.

  • Each alert has a priority, a countdown, and a business impact.
  • Choose an action for each alert before its timer runs out.
  • The right action improves KPIs and Score. A wrong or ignored call damages KPIs, raises cost, and hurts customer satisfaction.
  • Some decisions have delayed consequences — watch the event log.
  • Alerts get more frequent and can overlap as the 3-minute shift goes on.
  • Survive the shift with the strongest KPIs and highest score possible.
