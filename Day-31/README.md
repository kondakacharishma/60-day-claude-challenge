# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to build an interactive AI Supply Chain Control Tower using Claude AI.
The application simulates the role of a Head of Operations, where users monitor real-time operational alerts, make strategic decisions, and improve key business performance indicators through an interactive control tower dashboard.

---

## 📦 Project Overview

AI Supply Chain Control Tower is an interactive operations simulation that allows users to manage supply chain incidents in real time. 
Users prioritize operational alerts, choose corrective actions, monitor business KPIs, and evaluate overall operational performance through a modern enterprise dashboard.

---

## ✨ Features Implemented

### 🚨 Operations Monitoring

- Real-Time Operational Alerts
- Priority-Based Incident Management
- Business Impact Analysis
- Live Event Log

---

### 📊 KPI Dashboard

- Service Level
- Customer Satisfaction
- Inventory Health
- Transportation Efficiency
- Operating Cost
- Revenue Protected
- Overall Score
- Countdown Timer

---

### ⚙️ Decision Management

- Expedite Shipment
- Use Backup Supplier
- Reroute Trucks
- Increase Production
- Transfer Inventory
- Approve Air Freight
- Delay or Ignore Decisions

---

### 📈 Performance Dashboard

- Final Operational Score
- Performance Grade
- KPI Summary
- Alerts Resolved
- Correct & Wrong Decisions
- Operational Summary
