# Day 24 – Business Strategy Report using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use Claude AI as an AI Co-Founder, Growth Strategist, YC Advisor, and Business Consultant to evaluate the business viability of a startup idea and generate a comprehensive Business Strategy Report based on the Day 23 Customer & MVP Blueprint.

---

## 💡 Startup Idea

**AI Career Readiness Platform**

An AI-powered platform designed to help students and fresh graduates improve their resumes, prepare for interviews, identify skill gaps, and receive personalized career guidance to become job-ready.
