# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to build an interactive Supply Chain Crisis Simulator using Claude AI. 
The application simulates real-world supply chain disruptions, allowing users to analyze business crises, make strategic decisions, negotiate with suppliers, answer executive leadership questions, and implement AI-driven strategies to improve business resilience.

---

## 📦 Project Overview

Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab is an interactive business simulation where users take on the role of a supply chain leader. 
The simulator generates a fictional company and a random crisis scenario, requiring users to make informed decisions that impact business performance, customer satisfaction, operational resilience, and profitability.
