# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use Claude AI as a Startup Product Manager and Customer Research Expert to transform the Startup Validation Report into a structured Customer & MVP Blueprint. The report focuses on identifying the ideal customers, defining the MVP, prioritizing product features, and creating an actionable roadmap for product development.

---

## 💡 Startup Idea

**AI Career Readiness Platform**

An AI-powered platform that helps students and fresh graduates improve their resumes, prepare for interviews, identify skill gaps, and receive personalized career guidance to become job-ready.
